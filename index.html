Crip-Intelligence

Crip Intelligence is a response to AI Foundation Model’s regurgitated, ground & chipped, dull fantasy, cycling societal biases against disability. Output stuck in inspiration porn and unable to understand the joy or fantasy of the disabled experience. It’s nothing new, look to film, tv, games & literature and try and find a space for crip-fantasy. Like most technology this says more about the companies that control it than the science, engineering & art that make it possible (The Real World of Technology).

This project as part of a crip-revolt plays a "serious game” or a TTRPG (table top role playing game) which uses make-believe to invert Techno-solutionism and start with two prompts:

"AI Foundation Models are disabled”
Disability can save society from technology”

There are big and impossible questions that get crushed by Capitalist Realism and we want to play, imagining how disability might destroy or rescue Foundation Models from the companies controlling them. Or perhaps more importantly how we care for each other as everything gets a little bit worse.
